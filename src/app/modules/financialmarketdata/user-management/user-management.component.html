About Us - Information Empowerment

Televisory is a web-based information platform on diverse asset classes covering Financial, Fixed Income, Transactions, Commodities and Economic Data.

We assist Funds & Investors, Financial Institutions, Family Offices, Accountants & Advisors and Corporations for better decision making and improved productivity.

Our goal is to provide this at the lowest cost, without the need for specific hardware. Our web-based platform can be used anywhere, anytime. We not only provide data, but also enable online analytics as well as the ability to download this data.

Televisory is founded by Shiv Dave and it is headquartered in Singapore and has a development center in India.
